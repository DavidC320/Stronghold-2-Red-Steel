StrongHold 2 Red Steel
by David Cruz
Version 0.2.1 Alpha

Description
StrongHold 2 is the Sequal to StrongHold a pen and paper game that was recreated in tkinter with verying succes. In this game you the player
will explore maps or zones to find better equipment to get ready to fight the main boss of the zone. This game will explore themes of Identity
and loss of Idenity.
