Races
    Determines what weaknesses a character has within the game.
    Sub races are what controls what sprite is used in game.

    Organic - Characters that are made of flesh and blood
        Weak to Ice
        This species includes
            Humans
 
    Inorganic - Characters that are made of metal and circuits
        Weak to Fire
        This species includes
            Walkers - bipedal robots

    Spiritual - Characters that are made of energy
        weak to air
        This species includes
            Souls - ghost of those passed anchored to the world
